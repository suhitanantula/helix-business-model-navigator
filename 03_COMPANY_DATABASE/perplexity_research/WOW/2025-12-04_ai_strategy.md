## Full Research Response

### Data Platform & Analytics Partnership
Retail major Woolworths chose TCS as a strategic partner to transform its data capabilities. They helped establish the foundations of a common data platform and onboarded key company data from sales, finance, and supply chain business. This resulted in a roadmap for realizing connected, reliable, and AI-driven retail experiences, capitalizing on the power of Google Cloud.

TCS partnered with Woolworths to establish a company-wide data lake as a single source of truth with self-service analytics, empowering employees across business units to create new value. All data is now contained in a modern, scalable environment to inform stakeholders on everything from pricing and shelf usage to delivery management and staffing requirements.

### Quantium Investment - $223 Million
Woolworths (ASX: WOW) secured the controlling interest of data science company Quantium for $223 million. The investment increased Woolworth's existing shareholding from 47% to 75% following a successful partnership where Quantium's data science has been integral to the success of the national retailer.

### 2025 AI-Driven Productivity Framework
Woolworths' 2025 "Coaching and Productivity Framework" epitomizes the growing reliance on AI to optimize operations. The system uses algorithmic scheduling, wearable surveillance, and real-time performance tracking, aiming to enforce "engineered standards" for warehouse workers.

### Digital & AI Initiatives (F24/F25)
The 2024 Annual Report mentions AI positioning the Group for its second century. Average weekly visits to Group digital assets increased by 19.7% with digital visits now exceeding store transactions. Budget-friendly digital tools and new features planned for F25 include Ask Olive, Watch Lists and others.

### Scan&Go AI-Powered Shopping
In August 2024, Woolworths launched a "digital revolution in grocery shopping" with the Scan&Go trolley - a smart shopping trolley that allows customers to control their budget by tracking their purchases in real-time.

### Awards Recognition
Woolworths Group won "Best Artificial Intelligence 2025" at the Retail Technology Awards Europe (reta awards).
