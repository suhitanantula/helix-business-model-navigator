### Technology Leadership Structure

**Angelo Clayton - Chief Digital and Technology Officer (CDTO)**
Woolworths Group has appointed Angelo Clayton as its new chief digital and technology officer (CDTO), following the departure of James Wilson at the start of the year. Clayton has been with the group for over seven-and-a-half years, most recently as its technology director for stores and information management.

**John Hunt - CIO & Director of Group Enablement**
John Hunt serves as CIO & Director of Group Enablement at Woolworths. He initially joined Woolworths Group in 2017 as Chief Information Officer and spent over 25 years at Woolworths Holdings, South Africa where he held a range of senior IT and core retail leadership roles, including CIO and Senior Executive for Food Planning and Value chain. John was appointed to the Quantium Board in May 2021.

**Steve James - GM of Technology (Woolworths New Zealand)**

Woolworths is a large organization, consisting of a very complex supply chain system, over 22,000 employees, and 190 stores in New Zealand alone.
